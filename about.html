About this app

A small nudge to sit up straight.

Posture Check was created to remind me to sit up straight. I hope it works for you too.

It is a simple installed web app: set an interval, start the timer, and let it nudge you with posture checks, stretches, movement breaks, eye relief, and quick mindful check-ins.

The app is intentionally local and lightweight. It has no backend, no account, and no feed to manage. Your reminder interval, sound choice, and light or dark mode preference are saved in your browser on this device.

What it includes
Reminder timer The circular countdown shows how long is left until the next check-in. Start, pause, or reset it whenever your work rhythm changes.
Quick session Set a time limit for a focused work block — 25, 45, or 90 minutes. During a session the reminder auto-restarts every X minutes without any input from you. When the session ends, you get a done notification and it stops.
Pause presets Press Pause to stop reminders for a fixed window: 30 minutes, 1 hour, until tomorrow morning, or 8 hours for a presentation. The pause persists across app closes and auto-resumes when the window is up.
Adjustable interval Move the slider bar left or right to choose how often reminders appear, from 5 minutes up to 60 minutes.
Habit Coach A 66-day habit tracker based on UCL research. Tracks your streak, shows which phase of habit formation you're in, and suggests an appropriate reminder interval for your stage.
Chime control Use the music-note button to turn the reminder chime on or off. When sound is on, the app plays a short three-tone chime with each reminder.
Light and dark mode Use the moon or sun button in the top bar to swap between dark mode and light mode. The app remembers your choice for next time.
Notifications If browser notifications are enabled, reminders can appear while the app is in the background. If notifications are off, the app still shows reminders while it is open.
